Legal

Privacy policy.

Effective July 11, 2026 · Questions to hello@clewa.com

Your costs, margins, designs and factory relationships are your competitive edge. This policy explains what we collect to run Clewa, what we do with it, and what we will never do with it — with a plain-language summary beside each section.

01What we collect

We collect what's needed to operate your workspace, and nothing speculative:

  • Account information — name, email address, brand name, password (stored hashed), and billing details processed by our payment provider.
  • Workspace content — the material you and your invited collaborators put into Clewa: tech packs, orders, costs, messages, documents, inventory records and contacts.
  • Integration data — data from services you explicitly connect (for example Shopify or email forwarding), limited to the scopes you grant.
  • Usage and device data — log data such as IP address, browser type and pages viewed, used for security and to keep the service working.
In plain languageWe collect what you give us to run your production, plus the basic technical logs any service needs. We don't buy data about you or scrape data from elsewhere.

02How we use it

  • To provide, maintain and secure the service — including translation of messages you send through Clewa and the AI features described in section 04.
  • To process subscription payments.
  • To communicate with you about your account, security and material changes to the service.
  • To understand aggregate product usage so we can improve Clewa.
In plain languageYour data is used to run your workspace — not to build advertising profiles, and not to be sold. Ever.

03What we never do

  • We never sell your data, or share it with advertisers or data brokers.
  • We never show your costs, margins or terms to your factory — or to any other brand. Factory guests see only the order they're invited to.
  • We never use one customer's private data to advise another. Cross-order intelligence runs within your own workspace.
  • We never hold or move your money, so we never collect the banking credentials a payments custodian would need.
In plain languageThe boundaries the product enforces are the same ones this policy promises.

04AI features

Clewa's intelligence features (briefings, drafting, anomaly checks, Ask Clewa) process your workspace content to produce answers for you. AI processing is performed under contracts that prohibit our model providers from training on your content. You can switch AI features off entirely; Clewa remains a full system of record without them.

In plain languageThe AI reads your data to help you, doesn't train on it, and has an off switch.

05Who we share it with

Only the subprocessors needed to run the service — hosting, payment processing, email delivery, translation and AI processing — each bound by data-protection agreements. We share data with the collaborators you invite (your team, your factory guests) according to the roles you set. We disclose data to authorities only where legally required, and we tell you when we're allowed to.

In plain languageA short list of service providers, the people you invite, and no one else.

06Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256). Access is role-scoped and logged, integrations use least-privilege revocable tokens, and every change in your workspace carries an audit trail. The full posture is described on Security & trust.

07Retention & export

We keep your data while your account is active. You can export every order, cost sheet, message thread and document to Excel or PDF at any time, on every plan. If you close your account, we delete your workspace content within 30 days, except where the law requires longer retention (for example, billing records).

In plain languageYour data is yours to take, and leaves when you do.

08Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under GDPR and similar laws), you may have the right to access, correct, export, restrict or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. Write to hello@clewa.com and a human will handle it — you don't need to cite the statute.

09Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in and the service secure, and privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate usage. We don't use advertising cookies or cross-site trackers.

10Changes & contact

If we change this policy in a way that matters, we'll tell you by email before it takes effect — no silent edits. Questions, requests or complaints: hello@clewa.com.
