About

Production is the labyrinth. Clewa is the thread.

The name, the person behind it, what we believe, and exactly how we make money — the things you'd want to know before trusting a tool with your whole operation.

clew/ kluː /noun
  1. A ball of thread or yarn.
  2. The word that, over centuries, became "clue" — a thing that guides you through a difficulty.
From the myth: Ariadne gave Theseus a clew before he entered the Labyrinth. He unwound it on the way in — and followed it back out.
The name

Every feature is the same promise, kept a different way.

Getting a garment made is a labyrinth: your money, your designs and your timeline in someone else's hands — often in another language, another time zone, another legal system — before you know what normal even looks like.

A clew doesn't slay the monster or walk the maze for you. It does something quieter: it means you can always find your way back — to what was agreed, to what changed, to what happens next. That's the product, and the standard we hold every feature to.

Why this exists
"I've sat on both sides of a production order. The hard part was never the making — it was not knowing what I didn't know until it got expensive."
— The founder of Clewa

The order that taught me most was the one that went wrong: the spec that drifted between a WhatsApp thread and an email chain, the invoice that didn't match what we'd shaken hands on, the deadline that slipped one polite reply at a time. None of it was malice. All of it was fifteen places holding one truth, badly.

Clewa is the tool I wish I'd had: one place where the agreement is written down and signed by both sides, where the money has gates, where the calendar counts backward from the day that matters — and where someone finally tells you what to check before it costs you.

What we believe

Six positions we won't trade away.

01

Calm is the feature.

Production is stressful because the truth is scattered and the clock is invisible. One current record, and software that watches time on your behalf, is worth more than another dashboard of charts. If Clewa makes you check less, it's working.

02

Your money never touches us.

Deliberately. Clewa verifies a milestone's conditions and tells you it's safe to pay — then you pay your factory directly. Nothing for anyone to hold, freeze or misroute, and no incentive of ours riding on your transaction.

03

The record beats recollection.

Every spec, price and change signed and dated by both sides. When bulk arrives at 300gsm instead of the agreed 340, it isn't a he-said-she-said — it's a documented breach, and you have the receipts.

04

AI is a layer, never a dependency.

Switch it off and Clewa is still a complete system of record. The intelligence — briefings, anomaly checks, drafted hard conversations — earns its place by being useful, not by being unavoidable.

05

Your data is yours to take.

Every order, cost sheet and document exports to Excel, anytime, on every plan. A tool you can leave easily is a tool you can trust with everything.

06

No invented proof.

You won't find fake testimonials, borrowed logos or made-up metrics anywhere on this site. We'd rather you judge us the honest way: open the live demo and click around.

The business, plainly

How we make money — and how we refuse to.

One

Subscription software

Simple plans, priced by the depth you use — never by seats, never by a percentage of your production. Your first order is free. See pricing.

Two

Clewa Sourcing

A professional, senior-led service for brands that don't have the right factory yet — priced openly, from $7,500 per category plus 6–8% of production placed. See how it works.

Deliberately not part of the model
No cut of your production. No transaction fees, no GMV percentage, no markup hidden in a unit price.
No holding your funds. We're software, not an escrow or a bank — your money moves directly between you and your factory.
No factory commissions. In sourcing engagements we're paid by you alone, so the recommendation is never for sale.
No selling your data. Your costs, margins and factory relationships are your competitive edge, not our inventory.

When the money is simple, the incentives are yours.

The people behind it

A small team that has sat on both sides.

Clewa is built by a small senior team that has run production orders as the brand and answered for them on the factory floor — across categories, countries and more than one near-disaster. That experience is the spec for everything here.

We keep the team small on purpose: the same people who design the product take the sourcing calls and answer support. If you write to us, someone who has placed a PO writes back.
