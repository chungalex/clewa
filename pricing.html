Billing, plainly

The questions people actually ask.

When does the free order end?+
When you close it — there's no clock. Run the order at your pace, from tech pack to delivery. When it's done, pick a plan to keep going, or export everything to Excel and walk away with your data.
Does my factory have to pay?+
Never. Factories join by one link, work in their own language, and pay nothing — on every plan. If they had to pay, they wouldn't join, and the shared record only works when both sides are on it.
Why isn't it priced per seat?+
Because the value scales with production, not headcount. Invite your whole team — design, ops, finance — without doing math about who's worth a login. Plans differ by depth: one order, the day-to-day, or the whole operation. Annual billing takes two months off.
Does Clewa take a cut of my production?+
No. No transaction fee, no percentage of order value, no markup, and we never hold or move your money — you pay your factory directly. The subscription is the whole business model. (Clewa Sourcing is separate, optional, and priced transparently above.)
Can I turn the AI off and still get my money's worth?+
Yes. With AI off, Clewa is still the full system of record — the timeline, the Record, safe-to-pay, inventory, exports. The intelligence is a layer on top, never a dependency, and never priced as a hostage.
What happens to my data if I cancel?+
You take it with you. Every order, cost sheet, message thread and document exports to Excel or PDF, anytime — while subscribed or on the way out. Read the full posture on Security & trust.
