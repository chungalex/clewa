Security & trust

You're trusting us with your whole operation. Here's what we do to deserve it.

Money, designs, agreements, factory relationships — everything that can go wrong in production goes through Clewa. So the trust model isn't fine print. It's the product. Five commitments, in plain language.

01

We never hold or move your money.

Clewa is not a payment processor, an escrow, or a wallet. Every payment goes from your bank to your factory's bank, directly — the same wire you'd send anyway. What Clewa adds is the moment before: the safe-to-pay check, which verifies that a milestone's agreed conditions are actually met before you send anything.

If the sample isn't approved, if QC hasn't passed, if the invoice doesn't match the record — Clewa tells you, before the money leaves.

We will never ask you to "release funds through Clewa." If anyone does, it isn't us.

02

Everything lives on the record.

Every spec, price, and term you agree with a factory is locked into a versioned record, signed off by both sides with a date. Every change afterwards is a new version — nothing is edited in place, nothing quietly disappears.

So when the bulk arrives at 300gsm instead of the agreed 350, it isn't a he-said-she-said across a language barrier. It's a breach of the record, and you have the receipts.

03

Nothing proceeds until it's been read.

Most production disasters aren't malice — they're a factory that skimmed. Clewa makes reading structural: your factory confirms each spec line individually, and unconfirmed lines block the next stage from starting. A read receipt on every line that matters.

04

Your factory sees only what you share.

Factories join as guests, scoped to the orders you invite them into. They see the specs, the timeline, the messages — never your margins, your other factories, or your finances. A hard boundary, not a setting you can get wrong.

05

Your data is yours.

Every table in Clewa exports to Excel, on every page — orders, costs, inventory, contacts, the lot. If you ever leave, you take a complete, usable copy of your operation with you. We keep you by being useful, not by holding your data hostage.

Your designs, tech packs, and prices are confidential business data. We don't sell them, share them, or show them to anyone you haven't invited.

The practical layer

The unglamorous things that keep the commitments true, day to day.

Encrypted, in transit and at rest

Your files, messages, and records are encrypted on the wire and on disk — standard, serious infrastructure.

Role-scoped access

Owner, production, design, factory guest — each role sees its slice and nothing more. Access is granted per order, revocable any time.

A dated trail of everything

Sign-offs, changes, confirmations, and payments-marked-safe all carry a timestamp and a name. Nothing important is anonymous.

Export everywhere

Excel export on every table. Full account export on request. Leaving is always an option — that's the point.

Versioned, never overwritten

Documents and records are versioned. An old tech pack, a superseded price, a changed date — all still there, all still dated.

Humans you can reach

Security questions get answered by the people who built it — not a ticket queue. Ask us anything before you commit.

Straight answers

The questions careful founders ask before moving their operation in.
If Clewa never holds money, how does it protect me from getting burned?+
By sequencing, not custody. Getting burned almost always means paying before conditions were met — a deposit before terms were locked, a balance before QC. Clewa gates each payment behind verifiable conditions (sample approved, QC passed, invoice matching the record) and tells you when it's safe. You stay in full control of your money; you just stop paying blind.
Can my factory see my margins or my other suppliers?+
No. Factory guests are scoped to the orders you share, and the boundary is structural: costing, margins, finances, and your other factory relationships are never rendered in a factory-facing view. There is no toggle to get this wrong.
What happens to my data if I cancel?+
You export everything — every order, record, cost sheet, and contact — in normal, usable formats (Excel, PDF). Then, on request, we delete your account data. No exit fee, no held-hostage archive.
Is my tech pack safe? Designs are my whole business.+
Your designs are encrypted, access-controlled, and visible only to your team and the factory guests you explicitly invite, per order. We never use your designs or business data for anything except running your workspace — not for marketing, not for other customers, not for training anything.
What does the AI see, and can I turn it off?+
The intelligence layer reads only your own workspace — your orders, dates, and records — to warn you about your own risks. It's a toggle: switch AI assist off and Clewa remains a complete system of record, no predictions, no suggestions.
