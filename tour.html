The exchange rate moved. Your cost didn't.

You quote in dollars; your factory thinks in euros, dong or lira. Between deposit and final payment, the rate drifts — and the invoice surprises you. Clewa locks the FX snapshot at PO, so your real cost in your currency stays fixed no matter what the market does. Drag the slider and watch the gap you'd have eaten.

The moment people get burned is the moment money moves before conditions are met. Clewa gates every payment behind the things you agreed — QC passed, invoice matching the record, packing confirmed — and holds a clear don't-pay-yet until each one is verified.
