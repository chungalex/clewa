01

Make & plan

Define the product, price it, place the order, and decide what to make next.

Tech-pack builder

Tab · Tech packs
Flagship

Half of all production mistakes start with a spec the factory had to guess at. Build it complete here, and a live check catches the gaps before they cost you a sampling round.

  • Garment details, BOM, measurements, labels & packaging
  • Completeness score + AI gap warnings ("no lining → extra round")
  • Artwork, CAD & reference image slots that travel with the spec

Orders

Tab · Orders

When specs live in email and prices in WhatsApp, nobody's sure what was actually agreed. Here every order keeps its whole story in one place — so the truth is never in doubt.

  • Stage tracker per order; tabs for Overview, Record, Samples, Messages, Docs
  • The Record — locked specs/price/terms, dual sign-off, change history
  • Change orders that re-version the record and need both sides to re-confirm

PO generator

Tab · PO generator

The purchase order is the moment it gets real. Build a clean, professional one in minutes, export it, and send it — no fragile spreadsheet, no terms left off.

  • Live-updating PO document as you fill the form
  • Deposit math, incoterms, terms & signature blocks built in
  • Export to PDF or send straight to the factory

Planning

Tab · Planning

The best brands decide what to make on purpose, not by reflex. Plan the line, set the budget, and move ideas from sketch to ready-to-make — all before a cent is committed.

  • Line plan — newness vs. carryover, price tier, target margin
  • Open-to-buy budget — what's committed vs. still free to spend
  • Design pipeline + projected spend across the months ahead

Contract drafting

Tab · Orders
Flagship

Nobody wants to write the manufacturing agreement — so most brands skip it, then have nothing to point to when it goes wrong. Clewa drafts a clear one from your order, so the terms are spelled out before work starts.

  • Auto-drafted from the agreed record — specs, price, terms, dates, remedies
  • Plain-language and bilingual, so you skip the awkward back-and-forth
  • When something slips, it's clear what was agreed and who's covered
02

Track

Time, money, quality and stock — the live state of everything in flight.

Calendar

Tab · Calendar
Flagship

Miss the date you needed to place the order and the whole season slips. Set your launch and Clewa builds the plan backward — around your factory's holidays — so you always know the real deadline.

  • Backward planner: set a launch, get the date you must place by
  • Timeline + month views; production & campaign lanes together
  • Factory closures (Tet, August breaks) + collision alerts

Finances

Tab · Finances

Production is where money quietly leaks. See everything you've committed, paid and owe in one view — with the exchange rate locked so the final invoice can't surprise you.

  • Season KPIs, margin-by-order, cash-out schedule
  • Live costing — landed cost & margin per style
  • Multi-currency with FX locked at PO; you pay factories directly

Inventory

Tab · Inventory
Flagship

You can't make 320 coats with buttons for 290 — and you can't sell what you've quietly run out of online. Clewa connects to your store, watches what's selling, and tells you when to reorder while there's still time to make it.

  • Shopify sync — live sell-through in, incoming production out
  • Reorder warnings — "Black tee sells out in ~3 weeks; a reorder takes 6 — start now"
  • BOM auto-deduction + wastage prediction ("short at unit 290")
  • Size-curve intelligence, allocation guardrails, deadstock & aging, live WIP & guided counts

Quality control

Tab · Quality

The scariest moment is paying for work you haven't seen. Inspect the same checklist as your factory, with photos — and it has to pass before the balance is ever due.

  • Shared checklist with photos, against the approved sample
  • AQL pass/fail gate; safe-to-pay verification before money moves
  • Returns/RMA loop — defect reasons feed factory scorecards
03

Communicate

Talk to any factory, in any language — and keep every word on the order.

Messages

Tab · Messages
Flagship

Language and time zones turn simple questions into week-long delays. Write in yours, they read in theirs, replies come back translated — and none of it gets lost in a chat thread.

  • Live two-way translation; originals saved to the record
  • One thread per factory, attached to the right orders
  • No app for them to learn — they keep working how they do

Inbox

Tab · Inbox

Your factory isn't going to stop using email — and they don't have to. Forward, paste, or connect, and their messages land on the order, translated and parsed. No inbox handover required.

  • Three ways in: paste it, forward to an address, or connect
  • Quotes, terms & dates read straight onto the order
  • AI-drafted replies in the factory's language

Shared workspace

Tab · Shared view

Most disputes are really just two people looking at different versions. Put you and your factory on one live order — same status, same dates — while your costs stay private.

  • Live status, dates & "whose move it is"
  • A clear "who sees what" boundary
  • Factories never see your costs, margins, or other factories

Contacts

Tab · Contacts

It takes years to learn which factory actually delivers. Clewa remembers for you — every maker and contact, with a track record that outlasts any one order or teammate.

  • On-time rate, defect rate & orders run, per factory
  • Specialty, MOQ, certifications & key contact with languages
  • Institutional memory you keep even as your team changes

04

Intelligence

A production director that watches every order — and switches off whenever you want.

Intelligence

Tab · Intelligence

You can't watch every order every day — Clewa can. It sweeps them overnight and hands you a plain-language briefing: what's urgent, what can wait, what's running fine.

  • Morning briefing, ranked by what it blocks
  • Readiness gate — production can't start until specs/BOM are complete
  • Anomaly watch (invoice ≠ record) + patterns from your history

Ask Clewa

Tab · Ask Clewa
Flagship

When you don't know what you don't know, you freeze or overpay. Ask in plain English and get an answer grounded in your own data, with its sources shown — like a veteran sitting beside you.

  • "Which factory for the new coat?" → answered from real scorecards
  • Every answer cites the data behind it — never a black box
  • One toggle turns all AI off — the system of record works without it
05

Library & team

The permanent record, the season's story, and everyone who works on it.

Documents

Tab · Documents

Specs, POs, invoices, inspection reports — scattered across inboxes and drives, they vanish exactly when you need them. Here it all lives in one place: easy to find, always current, with a full trail of every version.

  • Organized by order and by type
  • Version history so you always have the latest — and the trail
  • Export anything to Excel — your data is always yours

Season close

Tab · Season close

Most brands finish a season and never really learn from it. Clewa compiles the story automatically — what shipped, what it earned, who delivered — so every season sharpens the next.

  • Units, retail value, on-time & blended margin at a glance
  • What went well / what slipped, plus factory grades
  • The report you'd forward to your team or investors

Team & roles

Tab · Team

As you grow, the right people need the right view — and your factories need a way in that doesn't expose your business. Give everyone their slice; keep your numbers yours.

  • Roles: owner, production, design — each their slice
  • Factory guests scoped per order, never your financials
  • Custom avatars so the workspace feels like real people

Ways of working

Everywhere

First order or your fiftieth, Clewa meets you where you are — teaching every term as you go, or getting out of the way when you already know the drill.

  • Guided vs. Pro mode, with plain-language coaching & a glossary
  • Customize panel — hide the tabs and tiles you don't use
  • Bring your own factory, local or overseas — geography is just a setting
