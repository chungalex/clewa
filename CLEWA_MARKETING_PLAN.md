## 1. TRUST & SAFETY (the emotional core — lead here) ★
| Feature | In app | How to portray on marketing |
|---|---|---|
| The Record — agreed specs/price/terms locked, dual sign-off, change history | ✓ | Hero-adjacent section. Show the locked agreement card with the negotiated-down price + green stamps. Line: "breach of the record — you have the receipts." |
| Safe-to-pay gate — verifies milestone conditions and tells you it's safe to pay (you pay the factory directly; Clewa never holds/moves funds) | ✓ ★ | Dedicated feature row with the deposit card + 4 green checks. The anti-scam moment. |
| Multi-currency / FX locked at PO | ✓ | Part of the safe-to-pay / finances row: "the euro moved 1.2%, your cost didn't." |
| Line-by-line factory acknowledgment (readiness gate) | ✓ | Inside "nothing starts incomplete" — production can't book until each spec is confirmed. |
| You keep custody of funds (Clewa never holds money) | ✓ | Trust chip in hero + reinforced in safe-to-pay. |

## RECOMMENDED MARKETING PAGE STRUCTURE (the reset)
1. **Nav** — Product · Pricing · About · Live demo · Sign in · Start free
2. **Hero** — "Follow the thread." + live product card (orders w/ statuses) + trust chips
3. **Proof strip** — "Any country · any language · you keep custody of funds"
4. **The problem** (dark) — 3 horror-story scenes with cost + the Clewa fix
5. **The Record** — locked agreement visual ★
6. **Safe-to-pay + FX** — the anti-scam gate ★
7. **Shared surface + translated chat** — same page, literally ★
8. **Intelligence + Ask Clewa + AI-off toggle** ★
9. **Inventory depth** — BOM/WIP/size-curve/deadstock ★
10. **The interactive dashboard mockup** — full current nav
11. **Guided vs Pro** — grows with you ★
12. **Capability grid** — everything else (12 cards)
13. **Clewa Sourcing** — paid service
14. **Security & trust** — NEW
15. **Pricing** — first order free
16. **Founder note + the myth (clew/Ariadne)**
17. **CTA** — "Follow the thread." + footer

Principle: lead with TRUST and SHARED SURFACE (emotional, differentiated), then INTELLIGENCE, then DEPTH. Don't open with feature breadth — open with the feeling of not getting burned.
