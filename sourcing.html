What this is

A paid, senior-run sourcing house inside Clewa: real people doing the brief, the audits, the negotiation and the onboarding — accountable to you alone, capacity-limited by design.

What this is not

Not a marketplace, not a directory, not a matching algorithm. No factory pays to be found by us, and no recommendation is ever for sale.

The engagement

Three stages, run by senior people.

The same discipline the software enforces, applied to finding your factory — everything documented in Clewa from the first call.

01

Brief & scope

A working session on your product and your reality — so we search for the factory you need, not the one that's easiest to find.

  • Product, construction and quality standards
  • Quantities today and where you're headed
  • Target cost and timeline, stated plainly
02

Search, audit & shortlist

Candidates identified from a vetted global network, then put through the checks most brands can't run from a distance.

  • Capability & compliance audit
  • Reference checks with brands they've produced for
  • Sample evaluation against your standard

You get a shortlist of three — with the evidence, not just names.

03

Negotiate & onboard

We negotiate price and terms on your behalf — people who know what normal looks like, on your side of the table.

  • Price, terms and MOQs negotiated for you
  • Warm introductions to the people you'll work with
  • Your first production run managed in Clewa

The relationship belongs to you — contacts, terms and history live in your Clewa, not ours.

Straight answers

Asked before every engagement.

Do factories pay you to be recommended?+
Never. No listing fees, no commissions, no kickbacks — we're paid by you alone. It's the only way a shortlist means anything: if a factory could buy its way on, the audit would be theater.
Who owns the factory relationship afterwards?+
You do, entirely. The contacts, the negotiated terms, the audit evidence and the order history live in your Clewa account. There's no lock-in clause and no ongoing fee for keeping the relationship.
What if nothing on the shortlist fits?+
Then we say so and keep searching — we don't force a fit to close an engagement. Capacity is deliberately limited so no one is ever pressured to accept the "good enough" option. If your category is one we can't serve well, we tell you before taking the retainer.
Do I need this to use Clewa?+
No. Clewa is built bring-your-own-factory — most brands invite an existing maker with one link and never talk to us. Sourcing exists for the moment you don't have the right factory, or your next product needs one you've never met.
Why is it priced so differently from the software?+
Because it's a different thing. The software is a subscription (see pricing — first order free). Sourcing is senior people spending weeks on your product: audits, references, samples, negotiation. Priced like the service it is, so it's never subsidized by cutting corners.
