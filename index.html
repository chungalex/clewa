Why it's scary

You're trusting a factory with your money, designs and timeline — before you know what normal looks like.

The first orders are where brands get burned. Not because factories are bad, but because no one's holding the whole picture. Here's where it goes wrong — and what Clewa does instead.

i.

It lives in fifteen places

Specs in email, prices in WhatsApp, the sample on someone's desk. By bulk, nobody's sure what was actually agreed.

One shared record both sides sign — versioned, dated, undisputable.
ii.

You find out too late

The wool comes back at 300gsm not 340. The drop date's blown. You learn the moment it's expensive to fix.

Clewa watches time and specs, and warns you weeks ahead.
iii.

You pay before you're sure

Deposit's out, the invoice is €300 over, and you've no leverage left. Money moved before reality did.

Clewa verifies every condition's met before you pay — you pay the factory directly.

Getting started
I've never produced before — is this too advanced for me?
It's built for exactly that. Switch on Guided mode and Clewa walks you through every step, defines the jargon as it appears (AQL, FOB, landed cost), and tells you what to do next — like having a production manager beside you. Outgrow it, and Pro is one click away.
How long does it take to set up?
Minutes to your first order. Start a style, invite your factory with one link, and you're working — no implementation project, no onboarding call required. Import an existing tech pack or build one in Clewa with the completeness check catching gaps as you go.
What can I make with it?
Clewa is built for garments, apparel, accessories, footwear and soft goods — the guidance, tech-pack fields, QC standards and BOM logic are specific to how clothing is actually made, not a generic project tool bent to fit.
I already use spreadsheets and WhatsApp. Why change?
Because those don't catch the mistake before it costs you — the slipped date, the short component, the invoice that's $286 over what you agreed. Clewa keeps the spec, money, samples and messages in one always-current place and flags problems weeks early. You can still export everything to Excel anytime.
Working with factories
Do I need my own factory?
No. Already have a maker, local or overseas? Bring them in with one link — no marketplace, no switching. Don't have one yet? Clewa Sourcing is a senior-led paid service that finds, audits and onboards the right factory for you.
Will my factory actually use it?
They barely have to. You send one link; they open their order in their own language — no account, no download. Prefer to stay on email or WhatsApp? Those messages land on the order too, translated. Nothing about how they work has to change.
Does it work for local production, not just overseas?
Completely. Local removes the language barrier — it doesn't remove the spec, timeline, costing and inventory complexity. The backward-planned calendar, BOM-linked stock and the shared record matter just as much for 200 coats made down the road as 2,000 made abroad.
What happens when something goes wrong?
That's the whole point of the record. Every spec, price and change is signed and dated by both sides, so if bulk comes back at 300gsm when 340 was agreed, it's a documented breach — not a he-said-she-said. Clewa can even help you draft a clear factory agreement up front, so the terms are unambiguous before work starts.
Money & trust
Does Clewa ever touch my money?
Never — and that's deliberate. Clewa confirms a milestone's conditions are actually met, then tells you it's safe to pay. You pay the factory directly, on your own terms. Nothing for us to hold, freeze or misroute — and no money-transmitter risk to inherit.
How does it handle different currencies?
You see your real cost in your own currency throughout. Clewa locks the FX rate at the PO, so a swing between deposit and final payment never quietly inflates your bill — the number you committed to is the number you pay.
Is the AI required? Can I turn it off?
Yes, you can switch it off entirely. Clewa works as a full system of record with the AI disabled — the assistance (drafting, predictions, the morning briefing) is a layer on top, never a dependency. Trust it at your own pace.
Plans & your data
Is my data secure, and can I leave?
Encrypted in transit and at rest, with scoped, revocable integrations and a full audit trail of who changed what. And you're never trapped: export every order, cost sheet and document to Excel, anytime. Your data is yours to take.
Can my team and store connect to it?
Yes. Invite your team with role-based access (design, ops, finance), and connect Shopify so sell-through flows in and reorder alerts count back the lead time for you. Factories get guest access scoped to only their order — never your costs or other makers.
What does it cost?
Your first order is completely free — no card, no clock; it ends when you close it. After that: Atelier at $59/mo for day-to-day production, Maison at $199/mo for inventory depth and intelligence, House on custom terms. Never per-seat, never a cut of your production. See pricing →
